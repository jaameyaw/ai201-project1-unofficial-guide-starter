# My school has no where for me to live on campus

Source: https://www.reddit.com/r/college/comments/wlbqbk/my_school_has_no_where_for_me_to_live_on_campus/

The poster says the school ran out of on-campus housing and asks what students do when they cannot afford off-campus housing or commute.

Common advice in the thread says to look for off-campus housing, use leftover financial aid for rent, ask for a landlord letter, and check for emergency housing or commuter alternatives.

Several comments also mention that schools sometimes over-admit students, put people on waitlists, or move students into temporary spaces like lounges, hotel rooms, or triple/quad occupancy rooms.

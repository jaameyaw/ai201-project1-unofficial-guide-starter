# Is dorm life worth it?

Source: https://www.reddit.com/r/college/comments/sx5l1a/is_dorm_life_worth_it/

Students compare the cost of on-campus housing with off-campus options and note that dorms can be expensive, especially when the school requires first-year students to live on campus.

The discussion suggests dorm life may still be worth it for convenience, but some commenters say off-campus housing can be cheaper even after accounting for meals and the value of the dorm experience depends on the school and the student's situation.

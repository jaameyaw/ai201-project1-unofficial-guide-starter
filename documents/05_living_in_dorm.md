# What is it Like Living in a College Dorm?

Source: https://www.reddit.com/r/college/comments/qamuez/what_is_it_like_living_in_a_college_dorm/

The thread discusses the dorm experience as a tradeoff between convenience and cost. Some students say first-year dorm requirements and scholarship rules can make on-campus living feel mandatory.

Other comments note that off-campus apartments can be cheaper and give more freedom, but the final choice often depends on meal costs, commute time, and whether a student wants more independence.

# Is student housing a scam??

Source: https://www.reddit.com/r/college/comments/leiasr/is_student_housing_a_scam/

The discussion compares student housing costs with dorm rates and says the price difference may be modest at some schools, but the overall market can still feel overpriced.

One viewpoint argues that the student housing premium is not always as bad as people think, while other comments point out that cost comparisons depend on whether utilities, location, and quality are included.
